# Patient Journey Wireframe ‚Äì Education-First Telemedicine Model

## Purpose of This Document

This document visualizes and explains the **end-to-end patient journey** for a
telemedicine startup using an **education-first, compliance-safe growth approach**.

It demonstrates how patients move from **health education** to **trust-building**
and finally to **voluntary telemedicine adoption**, without aggressive or non-compliant marketing.

---

## Core Journey Philosophy

> Patients should understand their health options **before** being asked to book a consultation.

This journey prioritizes:
- Education over promotion
- Trust over urgency
- Consent over coercion

## Detailed Journey Stages

### 1. Awareness (Top of Funnel)

**Goal:** Introduce the brand as a health educator, not a sales platform.

**Touchpoints**
- SEO blog articles
- Social media educational posts
- Community health talks
- Webinar promotions

**Patient Mindset**
> ‚ÄúI want to understand this better.‚Äù

**Compliance Notes**
- No condition-based claims
- No diagnostic language
- Public health education framing

---

### 2. Education (Health Literacy Stage)

**Goal:** Improve patient understanding and reduce anxiety.

**Touchpoints**
- Blog posts on symptom awareness
- Doctor-led educational webinars
- Short explainer videos
- Downloadable health guides

**Patient Mindset**
> ‚ÄúThis feels informative and trustworthy.‚Äù

**Compliance Notes**
- Clear educational disclaimers
- No personal medical advice
- Non-promissory language

---

### 3. Trust Building (Authority & Transparency)

**Goal:** Show credibility and ethical standards.

**Touchpoints**
- Doctor credentials and profiles
- Data privacy & security explainers
- Transparent care process documentation
- Recorded webinars

**Patient Mindset**
> ‚ÄúThese people seem qualified and ethical.‚Äù

**Compliance Notes**
- Verified credentials only
- No exaggerated claims
- Transparent limitations of telemedicine

---

### 4. Soft Conversion (Voluntary Action)

**Goal:** Allow patients to opt into care when ready.

**Touchpoints**
- ‚ÄúLearn more‚Äù CTAs
- App signup pages
- WhatsApp or email follow-ups
- Optional consultation booking

**Patient Mindset**
> ‚ÄúI‚Äôm comfortable taking the next step.‚Äù

**Compliance Notes**
- No urgency or fear-based language
- Consent-based data capture
- Clear opt-out options

---

### 5. Care Delivery (Telemedicine Consultation)

**Goal:** Deliver safe, professional virtual care.

**Touchpoints**
- Virtual consultation
- Follow-up educational emails
- Care navigation resources

**Patient Mindset**
> ‚ÄúThis experience feels professional and safe.‚Äù

**Compliance Notes**
- Clinical standards followed
- Documentation maintained
- Privacy and data protection enforced

---

### 6. Care Continuity & Retention

**Goal:** Maintain long-term trust and engagement.

**Touchpoints**
- Preventive health education emails
- Follow-up webinars
- Educational content recommendations

**Patient Mindset**
> ‚ÄúI trust this platform for future care.‚Äù
