# Doctor-Led Educational Webinar Outline

## Webinar Goal
Educate patients on health awareness and how telemedicine works,
without diagnosing or promoting specific treatments.

---

## Webinar Title Examples
- “When Should You Talk to a Doctor? A General Health Guide”
- “Understanding Telemedicine: What It Can and Can’t Do”
- “Navigating Care Safely in a Digital World”

---

## Target Audience
- First-time telemedicine users
- Busy professionals
- Parents and caregivers

---

## Webinar Structure (45 Minutes)

### 1. Welcome & Disclaimers (5 mins)
- Educational purpose statement
- No diagnosis or medical advice disclaimer
- Introduction of the doctor (credentials only)

---

### 2. Health Education Segment (15 mins)
- Common symptoms people ask about
- When professional care is typically recommended
- Preventive health awareness

---

### 3. Telemedicine Explained (10 mins)
- What telemedicine is
- What it is suitable for
- What it is not suitable for

---

### 4. Live Q&A (10 mins)
- General, non-personal questions only
- No personal medical advice

---

### 5. Next Steps (5 mins)
- Educational resources
- How to learn more about the platform
- Optional app signup (soft CTA)

---

## Compliance Notes
- No personal health questions answered
- No treatment guarantees
- All sessions recorded and documented
