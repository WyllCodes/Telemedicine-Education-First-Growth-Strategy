# Education-First Content ROI Dashboard

## Purpose
This dashboard tracks the effectiveness of educational content in driving
engagement, trust, and eventual telemedicine consultations.

Metrics prioritize **quality and intent**, not just volume.

---

## Funnel Overview

Educational Content
↓
Webinar / Email Engagement
↓
App Signup
↓
Consultation Booking

---

## Key Metrics Tracked

### Awareness Metrics
- Blog page views
- Video watch time
- Webinar registrations

### Engagement Metrics
- Webinar attendance rate
- Email open & click-through rates
- Time spent on educational pages

### Conversion Metrics
- App installs from education funnels
- Consultation booking rate
- Cost per educated lead
